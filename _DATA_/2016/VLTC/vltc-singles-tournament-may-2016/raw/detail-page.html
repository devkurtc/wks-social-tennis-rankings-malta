VLTC Singles Tournament May 2016

The tournament is scheduled to start on 14th May, 2016.  Matches will be played on Saturdays, Sundays and Public Holidays. The participants will be grouped into divisions according to their tennis ability and comparable skill level.  Matches will be played on a ‘best out of three sets’ basis with the 3rd set being played on an extended tie break (till 10).  Tie break at 6 all.
