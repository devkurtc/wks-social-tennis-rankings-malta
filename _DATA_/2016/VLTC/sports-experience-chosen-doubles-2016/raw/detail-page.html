Sports Experience Chosen Doubles 2016

The Vittoriosa Lawn Tennis Club will be organising its annual Men Ladies Doubles (chosen) Tournament between May and  June.   This tournament is scheduled to start on 14th May, 2016.  Matches will be played on Saturdays, Sundays and Public Holidays.   This year’s event is being sponsored by Sports Experience - Trophies and Engraving.  

 

Participants are required to indicate their partner’s name and details on the application form.   The participants will be grouped into divisions according to their tennis ability and comparable skill level so they can get the most enjoyment out of the game.   

 Matches will be played on a ‘best out of three sets with the 3rd set being an extended tie break (10).
