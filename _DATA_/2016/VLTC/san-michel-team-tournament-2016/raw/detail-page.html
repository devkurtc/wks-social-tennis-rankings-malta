San Michel Team Tournament 2016

 

Last Updated 16/01/2015 - 23:00

San Michel Team Tournament will start on Friday 15th January 2016. The tournament will be played on Fridays and Saturdays. Sundays and Public holidays may be used if required.

This tournament will be played on a two sets basis and all players will play a minimum of 6 matches.

All paid up members over the age of 15 are eligible to take part.

Participation fee is €18 which should be paid either online or directly at the club by Sunday 10th January 2016.

CLOSING DATE: WEDNESDAY 6TH JANUARY 2016.
