Wilson Autumn Team Tournament 2012

The Wilson Autumn Team Tournament 2012 will be held between 24th October - 7th December.  The tournament will be played on Wednesdays and Fridays.

Applications are being accepted at the club. Closing date for submission of applications is Sunday 7th October 2012.

Ladies will play on Wednesdays while Men will play on Fridays. Play starts at 6.30pm both on Wednesdays and Fridays. The rubbers shall be alternated in such a way that players will not always play at the same time each week. If required, semi-finals and finals will be played on a Saturday and Sunday.
