Wilson Autumn Team Tournament 2014

The Wilson Autumn Team Tournament is going to be held during October and December. The tournament, which is kindly being sponsored by Wilson, shall start on Wednesday 22nd October 2014. The tournament shall be played on Wednesdays and Fridays. Ladies will play on Wednesdays while Men will play on Fridays. Play starts at 6.30pm both on Wednesdays and Fridays.Closing date for for entries is 5th October 2014. Applications will be accepted on a FIRST COME FIRST SERVED basis.
