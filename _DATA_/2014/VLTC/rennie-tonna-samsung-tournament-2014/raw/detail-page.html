Rennie Tonna Samsung Tournament 2014

The Rennie Tonna Samsung Team Tournament sponsored by Sound Machine starts on Wednesday 23rd July 2014. Matches will be played on Wednesdays and Fridays, starting from 6.30pm. Sundays and Public Holidays may be used if required. Tournament will be played on a 2 sets basis. Applications will be on a first come first served. Closing date for entries: Friday 11th July 2014
