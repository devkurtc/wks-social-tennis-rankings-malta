San Michel Team Tournament 2014

San Michel Team Tournament which is being sponsored by San Michel Table Water, will start on Friday 17th January 2014. Matches will be played on Saturdays starting from 2.30pm. Sundays and Public Holidays may be used if required. The tournament will be played on a 2 sets basis (Tie-break at 6-6) and all players will play a minimum of 6 games. Closing date for entries Sunday 5th January 2014.
