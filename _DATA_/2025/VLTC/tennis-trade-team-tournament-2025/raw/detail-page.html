Tennis Trade Team Tournament 2025

Schedule of Play (Please note that dates are provisional and can be changed subject to multiple factors)

Day 1 – 29 & 31 October

Day 2 – 5 & 7 November, Day 3 – 12 & 14 November

Day 4- 19 & 21 November, Day 5 – 26 & 28 November

Semis 3 & 5 December

Final 12 December

The Vittoriosa Lawn Tennis Clubwill be organising its next team tournament during the months of November to December. The tournament, which is kindly being sponsored by Tennis Trading, shall start on Wednesday 29 October 2025.

The tournament shall be played on Wednesdays and Fridays.  Play starts at 6.30pm on both days.

All participants would be grouped in teams by the captains involved.

This tournament shall be played on an 18-game basis and players shall play in at least 3 encounters.

Applications will be accepted on a FIRST COME FIRST SERVED basis. All paid up members over the age of 14 are eligible to play. Applicants with too many unavailable dates as per the above will be subject to acceptance by the committee.  It is imperative that all players indicate their unavailable dates prior to the captains’ team selection. 

Participation fee is €15 which must be paid with the application.

CLOSING DATE FOR ENTRIES IS WEDNESDAY 15 October 2025, or until the amount of players required is reached.

The committee reserves the right to refuse any application without stating the reason.
