Seniors One Hundred Plus Doubles Day Tournament 2025

The VLTC will be once again organising a Seniors Hundred Plus Doubles Day Tournament. This tournament is to be held on Saturday 22 November for both males and females.  Another Saturday will be used if the number of particpants exceeds the amount requried.   Saturday Matches will commence at 9am.

This chosen partners tournament requires each doubles team to have a combined age of 100 and over (100+), therefore both players must be aged 50 or older.

 

Entry Fee is  €10.00 per Partecipant
