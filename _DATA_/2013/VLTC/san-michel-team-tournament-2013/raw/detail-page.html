San Michel Team Tournament 2013

San Michel Team Tournament which is being sponsored by San Michel Table Water, will start on Saturday 19th January 2013. Matches will be played on Saturdays starting from 2.00pm. Sundays and Public Holidays may be used if required.

The tournament will be played on a 2 sets basis (Tie-break at 6-6) and all players will play a minimum of 6 games.

Closing date for entries Sunday 6th January 2013.
