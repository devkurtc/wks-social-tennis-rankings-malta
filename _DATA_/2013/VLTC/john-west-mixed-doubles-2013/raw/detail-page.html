John west Mixed Doubles 2013

The John West Mixed Doubles Tournament is held between October and December.  This tournament is once again being sponsored by John West.The tournament is scheduled to start on Saturday 12thOctober 2013.  Matches will be played on Saturdays, Sundays and Public Holidays.

The tournament is being held on a round robin basis.  Couples will be grouped according to their ability and previous tournament results.Matches will be played on a ‘best of three sets’ basis with the third set being played by a tie break.  (Tie break at 6 all).

Closing date for entries 29th September 2013.
