Rennie Tonna Samsung T'ment 2013

The Rennie Tonna Samsung Team Tournament sponsored by Sound Machine starts on Friday 12th July 2013. Matches will be played on Fridays and Mondays, starting from 6.30pm. Sundays and Public Holidays may be used if required.

Tournament will be played on a 2 sets basis.

Applications will be on a first come first served basis.

Closing date for entries: Sunday 30th June 2013.
