San Michel ITF Under 18

The San Michel ITF U18 tournament starts on Saturday 19th March, with qualifiers with a draw of 32 boys and 32 girls. Main draw starts on Monday 21st March with a draw of 48 boys and 48 girls. There are also boys and girls doubles with a draw of 24.

Tournament finishes on Saturday 26th March with the boys and girls singles final.
