50 & Over Team Tournament

The Vittoriosa Lawn Tennis Club will be organising a novel 'Over Fifties' Team Tournament between October and November. 

The tournament is scheduled to start on Saturday 17th October 2015.  Matches starting at 4.00 pm will be played on Saturdays during the months of October and November.

This tournament will be played over two rounds and each rubber will consist of 2 sets. Each player will play a minimum of 4 matches. Applications will be accepted on a first come first served basis.

The club is also launching a new feature allowing you to book for tournaments online.

All paid up members who will be 50 (by the end of this year) and over are eligible to participate. Members failing to pay the membership fee will not be accepted in the tournament.

CLOSING DATE FOR ENTRIES:  FRIDAY 9th OCTOBER 2015
