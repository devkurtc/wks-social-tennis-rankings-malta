Rennie Tonna Samsung Team Tournament 2015

Updated 16/10/2015

 

 

The 2015 installation of the Rennie Tonna Samsung Team Tournament has been won by Team F, captained by Grace Barbara. Congatulations to the winners, runners-up, and all participants making this tournament another success.


The Rennie Tonna Samsung Team Tournament sponsored by Sound Machine starts on 22nd July 2015.
