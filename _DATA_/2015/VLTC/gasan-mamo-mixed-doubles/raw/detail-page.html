Gasan Mamo Mixed Doubles

The Mixed Doubles Tournament sponsored by Gasan Mamo Insurance starts on Sunday 25th October 2015. The tournament will be played on Saturdays, Sundays and public holidays starting at 3.30pm. This tournament is being held on a round robin basis.

Closing date for applications 16th October 2015.
