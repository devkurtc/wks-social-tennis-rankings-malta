Sports Experience Chosen Doubles 2026

The Vittoriosa Lawn Tennis Club will be organising its annual Doubles (chosen) Tournament between June and August. This tournament is scheduled to start on 15th June.  Matches will be played on Mondays. 

 

Participants are required to indicate their partner’s name and details on the application form.   The participants will be grouped into divisions according to their tennis ability and comparable skill level so they can get the most enjoyment out of the game.   

 

Matches will be played on a ‘best out of three sets with the 3rd set being an extended tie break (10).  This tournament will be played using the deciding point system.  Fixtures for all the matches together with a list of the tournament rules will be posted on the club’s notice board and website prior to the start of the tournament.

 

Tennis balls are to be provided by the players indicated on the left-hand side of the fixture list.

 

Postponed matches (except those rained off) must be played within 15 days of the scheduled date otherwise points will be forfeited by the player requesting the postponement. The player requesting the postponement is responsible for setting an alternative date and time for the postponed match to be played. Postponed matches (except those rained off) must be played on Saturdays or Sundays between 4.30 and 7.30pm.  Payments of courts for postponed matches shall be at the expense of the player requesting the postponement. The tournament Referee shall have a final decision on postponed matches.

 

Postponements due to bad weather will have to be recovered during the tournament on days indicated by the Tournament Referee.

 

All paid up members are eligible to participate. The participation fee is €15 per player.   This fee must be paid before the start of the tournament as otherwise the application will not be accepted.
