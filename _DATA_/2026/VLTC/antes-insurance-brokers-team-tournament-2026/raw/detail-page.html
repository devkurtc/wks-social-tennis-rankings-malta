Antes Insurance Brokers Team Tournament 2026

Played on Wednesdays and Fridays.

Play starts at 6.30pm both on Wednesdays and Fridays. 

Played in a two-set format using the deciding point system. All players will play a minimum of three matches and a maximum of four.

The participation fee for this tournament is €15.00 which should be paid online before the tournament starts.

All paid up members over the age of 14 are eligible to play. Applicants with too many unavailable dates will be subject to acceptance by the committee.

CLOSING DATE FOR ENTRIES IS FRIDAY 02 May 2026 or until the required number of players is reached.
