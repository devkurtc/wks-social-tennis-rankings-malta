H & S Solutions Singles Tournament 2026

 

The Vittoriosa Lawn Tennis Club will be organising its annual Singles Tournament between February and April 2026.

 

The tournament which is being kindly sponsored by H & S Solutions is scheduled to start on Wednesday 27 February 2026.  Matches will be played on Wednesdays (7pm or 8.30pm) and Sundays (4pm or 5.30pm) Players will be placed in divisions according to their level of play.  

 

Tournament will be played in a round robin format with matches being played on a ‘best out of three sets’ basis with the 3rd set being played on an extended tie break (till 10).  Normal Tie break is at 6 all.

 

Postponed matches (except those rained off) must be played within 15 days of the scheduled date otherwise points will be forfeited by the player requesting the postponement. The player requesting the postponement is responsible for setting an alternative date and time for the postponed match to be played.  Payments of courts for postponed matches shall be at the expense of the player requesting the postponement. The tournament Referee shall have a final decision on postponed matches.

 

Postponements due to bad weather will have to be recovered during the tournament on days indicated by the Tournament Referee.

 

Tennis balls are to be provided by the player indicated on the left-hand side of the fixture list.

 

All paid up members are eligible to participate. The participation fee is €15.   This fee must be paid before the start of the tournament as otherwise the application will not be accepted.
