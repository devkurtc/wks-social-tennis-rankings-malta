SAN MICHEL TEAM TOURNAMENT 2026

The Vittoriosa Lawn Tennis Club will be organising its next team tournament between January and April 2025.    The tournament, which is being sponsored by San Michel, will start on Friday 23 January 2025.  Matches will be played on Fridays evenings and Saturdays afternoon. Sundays and Public Holidays may be used if required.

This tournament will be played on a 2 sets basis and all players will play a minimum of 6 matches and a maximum of 8.  Die Point with the second deuce is being introduced. All paid up members over the age of 14 are eligible to play.

 The participation fee for this tournament is €20.00 which should be paid online before the tournament starts.
